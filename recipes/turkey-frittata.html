Turkey Frittata

Description

Frittata: A simple Italian egg dish, similar to an omelette or scrambled eggs. This dish is often made with whichever ingredients you have laying around. The only necessity is eggs and at least one filling.

This take on the dish keeps it simple yet delicious. Feel free to forgo or add ingredients at your discretion. Never stress the specifics of the ingredients when making a Frittata, they nearly always turn out great.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 2 thick slices of turkey
  • 2 medium cloves of garlic
  • 1/2 shallot
  • 1 tsp chili oil
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Cilantro or parsley to taste
  • Coarse or flakey salt to taste
  • High quality parmesan cheese to taste

Steps

  1. In a small-medium frying pan add 1 tbsp olive oil and place over low-medium heat.
  2. Chop up your turkey into ~1cm squares and add to frying pan.
  3. Mince your shallots and garlic while making sure to stir your turkey.
  4. Once turkey is sufficiently browned add your chili oil, shallots, and garlic.
  5. Crack your eggs and scramble them, making sure to keep an eye on your pan.
  6. Once the shallots and garlic are just barely browning remove the pan from heat.
  7. Transfer the contents of the pan to the bowl of eggs and stir.
  8. Add 1 more tbsp of oil to the pan and turn the temperature to medium.
  9. Add your bowl of eggs and fillings to the pan and scramble.
  10. Once eggs are sufficiently scrambled but still runny flatten the mixture then cover the pan.
  11. While eggs are finishing up chop your cilantro or parsley.
  12. After a few minutes the frittata should be just barely runny on top. At this point throw your greens atop.
  13. If the frittata is too runny, flip it and let it cook for a minute. Keep in mind egg dishes continue to cook after removed from heat.
  14. Slide the frittata onto a plate, top it with freshly grated parmesan cheese and flakey salt.
  15. Slice into your frittata like a pizza if you're sharing or simply dig in if it's just for you!
  16. Enjoy!
