Turkey Frittata

Description

A simple Italian egg dish, similar to an omelette or scrambled eggs. This dish is often made with which ingredients you have laying around. The only necessity is eggs and at least one filling.

This take on the dish keeps it simple yet delicious. Feel free to forgo or add ingredients at your discretion. Never stress the specifics of the ingredients when making a Frittata, they nearly always turn out great.
